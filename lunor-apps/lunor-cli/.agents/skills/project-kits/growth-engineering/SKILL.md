# Growth Engineering

## Instructions

1. Start from the business metric and user stage.
2. Compose analytics, CRO, SEO, content, RevOps, and product strategy work instead of treating growth as only copy.
3. Require instrumentation before experiments.
4. Keep experiments reversible and scoped.
5. Report decisions with metric, hypothesis, change, owner, and verification.
