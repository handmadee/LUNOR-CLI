# Data Science Research

## Instructions

1. Define the research question, dataset lineage, assumptions, and success metric.
2. Separate exploration, modeling, evaluation, and reporting artifacts.
3. Track data cleaning decisions and leakage risks.
4. Require reproducible environment, seed handling, and saved outputs.
5. Explain statistical uncertainty and limitations.
