# Compliance Readiness

## Instructions

1. Identify regulated data, access paths, retention rules, and audit requirements.
2. Map controls to evidence: logs, configs, tests, approvals, and runbooks.
3. Avoid legal conclusions; surface engineering gaps and decision points.
4. Check data minimization, consent, export, deletion, and incident response.
5. Produce an owner/action matrix for unresolved controls.
