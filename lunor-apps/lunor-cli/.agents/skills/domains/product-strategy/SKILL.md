# Product Strategy

## Instructions

1. State the user, problem, current workaround, and desired business outcome.
2. Separate strategy, scope, solution, metrics, and rollout.
3. Define non-goals and tradeoffs explicitly.
4. Tie features to activation, retention, revenue, or risk reduction.
5. Produce acceptance criteria that engineering can test.
