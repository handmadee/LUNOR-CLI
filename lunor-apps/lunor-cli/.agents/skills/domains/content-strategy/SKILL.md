# Content Strategy

## Instructions

1. Anchor content in audience, promise, proof, and desired action.
2. Separate positioning, messaging, editorial, and distribution concerns.
3. Preserve brand voice with examples, banned phrases, and tone ranges.
4. Design reusable templates for repeated content types.
5. Add review criteria for accuracy, originality, claims, and attribution.
